Synopsis

A story about an elf mage who outlives her companions and continues her journey through the world, learning about life and death along the way. After the party of heroes defeated the Demon King, they all went their separate ways. Frieren, being an elf, has a much longer lifespan than her companions, and after many years, she begins to reflect on the time she spent with them.
